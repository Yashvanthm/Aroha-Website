Know Us

Vaibhav Group designs and develops real estate properties across all segments and strives to make a difference to the way you live, work, entertain and leisure.

Vasathi Vaibhav Projects LLP, is into land development have aims to cover North Bangalore. We are currently conceptualizing a 20-acre Gated Community in Bengaluru, Karnataka. Delivering at most satisfaction and an unwavering dedication for transparency in dealings and ethical norms of business conduct. Each Vaibhavite is Bounded by Trust. Vasathi Vaibhav is a privately-held, full service real estate company located in Bangalore, Karnataka. With focus in North Bangalore, the fast appreciating region near New International Airport. Vasathi Vaibhav is way ahead among competition. With an urge to provide service, reliability, accessibility and values, Vasathi Vaibhav is committed to remain sensitive to the natural surroundings and provide the best amenities, breath-taking view & standards to its customers.
